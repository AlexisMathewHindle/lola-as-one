What if I need to change my booking, cancel or if my child is unwell?

Our workshop requires a 48-hour cancellation notice to ensure a full refund or rescheduling. Cancellations made less than 48 hours before the scheduled workshop will not be eligible for a refund. This policy allows us to manage resources effectively and offer spots to other participants. Please note if your child does not attend due to illness these rules still apply.
